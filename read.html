What Happened To Youth? The Impact of Social Media

Social Media

“Social Media” is playing a major role in ultramodern generation and providing the best aid to audience. It is known that each program has its own benefits and cons, whereas social media has also made people to improve their knowledge and skills but on another side, it is leading the audience to get distracted from their goals. Social media is used by every age-group or segment whether a student or a retired government employee. Students are very busy in playing games, using social platforms like Instagram, Facebook, You-tube, and Snapchat. These all applications are distracting the youth from their goals and putting effect on their emotional and social health. In relation, to understand the impact of social media on youth let’s understand its pros and cons.

Positive Impact

Enhance Communication : It helps the students to connect with others in positive way and can exchange the information with their peers, teacher, and friends and can work on their projects.
Information Sharing: The social media has allowed the audience to share the information world-wide which improves the knowledge of the students and can learn, read and grow.

Negative Impact

Distraction: Nowadays, students are concentrating on using the SM tools more than on their studies.
Loosing social confidence: Due to digitalisation, social media has decreased the confidence of the students to have a social connection with anyone as they are loving to stay away from social connectivity.

Conclusion

Social media has its positive and negative effects on youth, but it is important for the students to know how to use the platform for their betterment. Parents should advice their students and teach them the difference between the right and wrong sides of the platform and make them responsible to use social media.
